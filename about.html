About

Isabella Bruni is an emerging artist from Toronto, Ontario. She is currently completing her Bachelor of Fine Arts from Western University in the spring of 2024, specializing in studio art.


Her artistic practice utilizes acrylic paint and textile collage to explore the transformative power of objects in the everyday through still life painting. Her work takes inspiration from interior design, specifically mid-century modern formations.


Isabella’s work has been exhibited in numerous shows throughout her time at Western University, including the Annual Juried Exhibition in 2022 and 2024. Isabella has also been a frequent participant in various USC Public Arts Commission exhibitions throughout the years. She is involved in teaching fine arts to children and young adults with special needs since 2021 and works alongside her mother as an interior designer while also managing all social media platforms. Isabella is on track to receive her degree in interior design after the completion of her bachelor’s degree in fine arts.
